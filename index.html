More About Me

I kept looking for sales jobs in Schengen nations, but I was unable to locate any that paid more, I then turned to looking for other methods to boost my income, and after doing some study, I discovered that I could have been a developer much earlier.
While I was still trying to understand web development, I met an amazing person along the way who turned out to be my partner and who was also a senior software engineer.
I began my transition around the middle of the year 2022. During this time, I watched certified paid courses from udemy and a number of other sources, including MDN, and I also taught myself how to code. It wasn't simple at first, but finally I was able to finish web development since I kept reading and responding to so much stuff.
